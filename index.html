Pleas from the Trees

Stop now before it's too late

Wood, it is a vast and valuable resource. It is used in building, fueling and crafting. We all use wood in some point in our lives, but where does it come from? It comes from trees, and how to get wood from trees? We cut them down. But throughout history, civilization has done cutting trees for wood too much, to the point of entire forests being wiped out, and it has happened for centuries, still relevant today. In fact, here is a statistic for Brazilian Amazon deforestation rates and think, “Are we doing too much?”

Efforts were made to combat this, such as Mr. Beast’s Team Trees, which is kind of a failure. But these are futile if the demand for wood goes strong. If wood is wanted, trees WILL be chopped.
